A New way to listen to Music.

The third-party way to listen to music on WearOS

Concept

A dedicated application that allows you to listen to music and audiobooks without needing your smartphone.

Key Features

  • Streaming & Offline Mode
  • Audio playback
  • Simplified Configuration with the Companion app.

Architecture & Design

Visual Identity

Design optimized for AMOLED screens: Pure black background (#000000) and Jellyfin purple (#BB86FC).

Data Flow

The phone retrieves an AccessToken and transmits it to the watch via the Wearable Data Layer API.

Data Model

Use of structured objects (MediaItem, Song, Audiobook) and a local Room database for offline mode.

Project Update: Jellyfin for WearOS

Hello everyone, I would like to share some news regarding the development of Jellyfin for WearOS. Temporary Hiatus For the time being, active development of the application is being temporarily paused. Due to my studies and other personal priorities, my schedule is currently very full, which means that new features and major updates will take longer to arrive. The Project is Not Dead. It is very important to clarify that this project is not abandoned. Creating a high-quality WearOS client for Jellyfin remains a goal that is close to my heart. I simply consider this period as a "standby" phase to allow me to come back stronger later.

What Continues:

If critical bugs are reported, I will do my best to fix them.

Feedback: Your suggestions and comments are always welcome. They will serve as my foundation once development resumes at full capacity.

The technical roadmap remains the same, and my ambition for the application has not changed.

Thank you very much for your understanding and your support. This is only a "see you soon."

— The Development Team
